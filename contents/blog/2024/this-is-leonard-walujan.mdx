**Main Logo** has a warm colour that represents as the main identity of **Leonard Walujan**.

**Grayscale Logo** has a grey colour that represents the external things of **Leonard Walujan** in the general, hobbies and interests section.

**White Background Logo** has a white colour on the background that represents the alternative name of **Leonard Walujan** in the general section.

**Black Background Logo** has a black colour on the background that represents the alternative name of **Leonard Walujan** in the hobbies and interests section.

Each colour in the logo serves a different purpose in terms of branding. However, the owner will remain the same.

**This is Leonard Walujan** tagline is created for authenticity of personal branding representation.
